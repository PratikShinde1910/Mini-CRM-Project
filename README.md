## ğŸ”„ Application Workflow

- Users are greeted with a **Dashboard** displaying lead statistics and quick insights.
- The **Leads section** allows users to view all leads with relevant details such as title, value, and status.
- New leads can be created by selecting a customer and entering lead-specific information.
- Leads are categorized by status (New, Contacted, Converted, Lost) for better tracking.
- The **Customers section** lists all customers with search and quick action options.
- Users can add, edit, or manage customer-related information.
- Visual charts provide a quick overview of lead distribution and performance.
- The **Profile section** allows users to manage account settings, privacy options, and application preferences.
